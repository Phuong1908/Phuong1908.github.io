Project Overview

Silkroad Origin Mobile aims to give players the original and nostalgic feeling of the Silkroad Origin game in the mobile world. Our team has been dedicated to recreating this classic experience for a new generation of mobile gamers.

My Contributions

  • Quest System

    Developed a linear quest system where completing one quest unlocks the next. Quests are completed by meeting specific conditions such as collecting items, defeating monsters, or reaching locations.

  • Alchemy System

    Implemented an item upgrading system, allowing players to enhance their equipment and resources.

  • Item Recommendation System

    Created a quick interaction pop-up for newly acquired items, facilitating easy opening, using, or selling of items.

  • Performance Optimization

    Investigated and fixed several memory leak issues, improving overall game performance and stability.
